Summer CAMP Registration

Registration will open on June 29th, 2025!

  • Do not register if you are not planning on sending your child or your child does not want to attend camp!
  • Do not pay until we confirm you have a spot.
  • If you have registered and expect that you will not be able to send your child(ren), please contact us to free up a spot for someone else.

DEADLINE: July 24, 2025!

Please fill out our online registration form below. You will have to fill out the information for each child (thus you may have to enter the information multiple times).

Please note, do not delay. Last year we reached capacity within 24 hours and quickly had to close registration and start a waiting list.

Once you've read the rules and requirements and you've registered and paid, you are all set for this year's camp, Insha'Allah. We will send reminder emails closer to the start of camp to ensure all are aware of what they will need to bring and the protocols at camp.

Your child(ren) will NOT be confirmed as participants unless camp fees are paid or arrangements to pay have been made. More rules here...

READ the registration form before you sign it.

If you wish to send a cheque or pay by cash please email us to make arrangements to mail it or pick it up. Full payment is required to permit campers to attend.

Paying for Summer Camp Fees

2024 Camp Rates:

Campers ages 9-16: $400.

Leaders-In-Training youth ages 17-19: $400. Max 10 boy & 10 girls.

We recommend email money transfer to MYOADMIN@GMAIL.COM, but will accept cash, credit cards & Paypal.

Cash must be received before camp begins. No exceptions please. Thanks for your understanding. Camp fees are not refundable.

PayPal charges a fee per camper, that is the reason for the difference in fee vs the PayPal form. If you wish to avoid the additional fees, contact us at myoadmin@gmail.com to arrange cheque or cash payment. If you wish to make an email money transfer, please contact us at myoadmin@gmail.com. Fees may apply.
